# about

hi! i'm not much a talker on the internet and primarily created this website for aesthetics! i've always spent a lot of time customizing my online/gaming profiles, and got tired of the modern, corporate look of websites, so i hopped on the indie websites train. enjoy your stay, and feel free to come back :)

# on this site

before creating the theme of this website, i thought about what mood and setting i'd like to create. 
i enjoy walking at night, a lot. when the world feels calm and everything slows down. there’s a quiet peace in looking up at the stars, like the rest of the world is asleep and it's just me, the night sky, and my thoughts. although i'm a huge overthinker, somehow, when i'm outside, it doesn't feel so overwhelming anymore. being outside makes me realize i don't have to carry it all alone. it’s a comforting kind of loneliness.
